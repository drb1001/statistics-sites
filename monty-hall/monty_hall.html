The Monty Hall Problem

Have you heard of the Monty hall problem?

The best way to understand it is to play!

You are on a game show, and you have a choice of 3 doors.
Behind one door is the big pize - a car.
Behind each of the other doors is the joke prize - a goat.

So, you need to pick a door to try to find the car...
